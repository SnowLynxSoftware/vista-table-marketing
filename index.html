Our Mission

Our Plan

To achieve our mission, our team will create a user-friendly app for restaurant owners, prioritizing easy menu creation, improving the customer experience, and ensuring compatibility with existing systems. We'll offer data analytics, reliable security, and robust support while constantly evolving to meet industry needs. Our goal is to empower restaurants in the digital era.

Frequently Asked Questions

  • When is the platform's expected release date?

    Vista Table doesn't have an official release date yet. The expected timeline will be around Q1 2024 for a soft launch of customers who signed up for early adoption.

  • What will the pricing model look like?

    The pricing model is subject to change before launch, but currently we plan to have a limited free tier--which is meant to be a free trial that lasts forever with limited features. There will be a BASIC tier with more features, no customization, and limited support (via Email)--and a PRO tier with the most features, customization, and priority support (via Email).

  • How can I help support development and give feedback or submit new ideas or features that the product should have?

    There will be more official channels of communications as we get closer to launch--in the mean time, the best way to stay involved is to sign up for the mailing list at the top of this page. We will send out updates and maybe some surveys where you can submit your own ideas as well.
